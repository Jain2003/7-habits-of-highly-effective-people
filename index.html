HABIT 1

Be Proactive

Paradigm

Ineffective: I am the product of my circumstances.
Effective: I am a product of my choices
Reactive Behavior

Reactive people allow outside influences(moods, feelings and circumstances) to
control their response.

Proactive Behavior

Proactive people pause to allow themselves the freedom to choose their response
based on principles and desired results.

HABIT 2

Begin With the End in Mind

Paradigm

Ineffective: I live by default.
Effective: I live by design.
Live by Default

The person goes according to the circumstances having having no idea where the end is.
Possibility of serious consequences from enforcement action that can be extremely costly.

Live by Design

Person plans in such a manner that the end is always visible, and the way is much proper.
People are more likely to work toward a goal they know and set performance standards.

HABIT 3

Put First Things first

Paradigm

Ineffective: I put urgent things first.
Effective: I put important things first.
Unoraganised

In this situation a person usually finds himself/herself in a clumsy condition
in which he/she tries completing random works thus not completing it effectively.

Organised

Organised people always follow a time table PUTTING FIRST THINGS FIRST.
Thus completing the requires on time and effectively.

HABIT 5

Seek first to Understand Then to be Understood

Paradigm

Ineffective: I listen with the intent to reply.
Effective: I listen with the intent to understand.
Intent to reply

If you listen with the intent to reply, you don't hear everything that is being said. The focus of the
conversation is singular rather than broader, perhaps opening up doors to other topics, features etc.

Intent to understand

These kind of people seek to understand what the speaker is trying to convey.He/She understands each
and every bit of the conversation thus put correct and non repeated points when its his/her turn to speak.

HABIT 6

Synergize

Paradigm

Ineffective: Its your way or my way, or a compromise.
Effective: Together we can create a better one.
Either you or me

It is a overall problem when moving from start-up to developing a team.When individuals try to
impose solutions to meet their own needs, which are often in conflict with the needs of the other.

Its all of us

Teamwork is the ability of team members to work together, communicate effectively, anticipate and
meet each other's demands, and inspire confidence, resulting in a coordinated collective action.

HABIT 7

Sharpen the Saw

Paradigm

Ineffective: I focus only on getting the golden egg.
Effective: I nuture the goose that lays the golden eggs.
Focus on Succes

Results-focused leaders are driven by their end goal. It is their concentration on achievement,
not on the means of routine operations that defines them. The way always remain unknown.

Focus on Process

Focus on the process. Break your task into smaller pieces and do one thing at a time and then move
on to the next piece. The road to any goal is a matter of taking one step after the next.
